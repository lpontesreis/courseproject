This is the code book to describe the variables:

These signals were used to estimate variables of the feature vector for each pattern:  
'-XYZ' is used to denote 3-axial signals in the X, Y and Z directions.

tBodyAcc-XYZ: time domain total body acceleration. The units are standard gravity units 'g'.
tGravityAcc-XYZ: time domain total gravity acceleration. The units are standard gravity units 'g'.
tBodyAccJerk-XYZ: time domain body linear acceleration. The units are standard gravity units 'g'.
tBodyGyro-XYZ: Time domain of the angular velocity vector measured by the gyroscope for each window sample. The units are radians/second.
tBodyGyroJerk-XYZ: Jerk signal of time domain body angular velocity. The units are radians/second.
tBodyAccMag: magnitude using the Euclidean norm of the three-dimensional time domain total body acceleration. The units are standard gravity units 'g'.
tGravityAccMag: magnitude using the Euclidean norm of the three-dimensional time domain gravity acceleration. The units are standard gravity units 'g'.
tBodyAccJerkMag: magnitude using the Euclidean norm of the three-dimensional Jerk signal time domain body linear acceleration.
tBodyGyroMag: magnitude using the Euclidean norm of the three-dimensional time domain angular velocity vector measured by the gyroscope. The units are radians/second
tBodyGyroJerkMag: magnitude using the Euclidean norm of the three-dimensional Jerk signal time domain body linear acceleration measured by the gyroscope. The units are radians/second
fBodyAcc-XYZ: Fast Fourier Transform of total body acceleration. The units are 1/g.
fBodyAccJerk-XYZ: Fast Fourier Transform of total gravity acceleration. The units are 1/g.
fBodyGyro-XYZ: Fast Fourier Transform of the angular velocity vector measured by the gyroscope.
fBodyAccMag: Fast Fourier Transform of the magnitude of total body acceleration. The units are 1/g.
fBodyAccJerkMag: Fast Fourier Transform of the magnitude of Jerk signal of body angular velocity. The units are 1/g.
fBodyGyroMag: Fast Fourier Transform of the magnitude of body angular velocity measured by the gyroscope. The units are seconds/radian.
fBodyGyroJerkMag: Fast Fourier Transform of the magnitude of Jerk signal of the angular velocity measured by the gyroscope. The units are seconds/radian.


The set of variables that were estimated from these signals are: 

mean(): Mean value
std(): Standard deviation
